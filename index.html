About Antique Brick

Since 1988, Antique Brick has been Michigan's premier source for authentic reclaimed Detroit red bricks. We work directly with demolition sites to salvage quality bricks from historic buildings across the region.

Every brick we salvage tells a story. These bricks were carefully crafted in past centuries and reclaimed by us. You deserve nothing less than genuine antique bricks for your project.

We ship nationwide to customers throughout the US and Canada. Whether you're a contractor restoring a classic home, an architect designing with character, or a builder looking for something authentic, we have what you need.
